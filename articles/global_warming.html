Global Warming (Creative Writing #10)

Is it cold or has summer become warmer?
I'm not quite sure but I am getting older
Shore on the rise and it's hotter in Burma
*Industry grinds* Corporations avoid large fines
Don't panic, everyone dies
**Sooner than others** Call that corporate lies
More floods, big surprise
Seaworld sucks, Telsa spaceship big bucks
Colonise the moon, a milli for a room
Almighty dolla, shoutout to Elon

'cause we're all doomed
